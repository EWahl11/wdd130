Overview

Purpose

The purpose is to make my buissness more visible to my target audience and build revenue. This would possibly attract new clients and make it easier for past clients to find information about my buissness

Audience

Who are the target customers? They are around 20-50 years old and seeking more adventure in thier life. They are interested in the outdoors and being active in nature. What do they want? They want adventure and to have fun and to have the simplest way to get there. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They expect to find pictures relevant to water rafting. General information and FAQ's. Possibly a way to book a trip.
